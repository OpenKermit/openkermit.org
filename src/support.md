Before asking for help or reporting a bug, please make sure to consult the [documentation](doc) and [FAQ](faq).

If you need help with Kermit, the best place to go is the [OpenKermit discussions](https://github.com/orgs/OpenKermit/discussions) page.

If you have a bug to report in C-Kermit, you may do so on the [C-Kermit issues](https://github.com/OpenKermit/ckermit/issues) page.
